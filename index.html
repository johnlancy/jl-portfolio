Martin Electric

A family-owned and operated business for 20+ years. Located in the the Maryland DC Virgina Metropolitan Area.

Martin Electric takes pride in being a part of the communities they serve. Beyond providing great electrical services, the company actively engages in community initiatives and supports local events. This community-centric approach made it clear that it was a excellent choice for JLX to work with Martin Electric.

JLX proudly redesigned their brand. We picked a colorway that looks electric while also maintaining class. The copper orange for conduction, and a steel gray for grounding.
